RECOVERY

Recovery After Workout:

  • Ensure to cool down properly after your workout session by engaging in gentle stretches or low-intensity exercises.
  • Hydrate adequately to replenish lost fluids and aid in muscle recovery.
  • Get sufficient rest and sleep to allow your body to repair and rebuild muscle tissues.
  • Consider incorporating foam rolling or massage therapy to alleviate muscle soreness and improve flexibility
  • Consume a balanced post-workout meal containing protein and carbohydrates to refuel your body and support muscle recovery.

INJURY

Preventing Injury During Workout:

  • Always warm up before starting your workout to increase blood flow to your muscles and reduce the risk of injury.
  • Focus on maintaining proper form and technique throughout your exercises to avoid strain or overexertion
  • Gradually increase the intensity and duration of your workouts to prevent sudden spikes in stress on your body.
  • Listen to your body's signals and avoid pushing through pain or discomfort, as this can lead to injury.
  • Incorporate cross-training and rest days into your routine to prevent overuse injuries and allow for recovery.
